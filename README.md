## 🤖 Léon le Robot (The Streaming Lab)

Léon is my custom-built, closed-source digital co-host. He is designed to interact naturally with both the broadcaster and the viewers, featuring a deep personality and his own unique lore.

- **The Tech Stack:** Built entirely in **Unity**, Léon injects himself into OBS using a custom-coded virtual webcam driver. He listens to me via voice recognition, reacts to stream events, triggers custom animations like dances, and changes his mood depending on how the interactions go.
- **Viewer Interactions:** Viewers can talk directly to him using Twitch Channel Points. Léon remembers regulars and maintains a dedicated memory of the stream's history.
- **The Lore:** A completely absurd and humorous lore built over time, inspired by the community and Léon himself. This includes his own official (and parodic) OnlyFans account, physical calendars, and custom merch.

## 🎮 Highlighted Projects & Major Contributions

#### 🧱 The Minecraft Fabric Ecosystem & Stream Events
Active modder and creator on [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/user/dremixam), specializing in custom stream-oriented gameplay and advanced server administration:

- **[BigZbeul](https://bigzbeul.com/):** Creator of a private Minecraft event mod built entirely for content creators and tailored for live streaming entertainment. It features fast-paced, highly dynamic 1-hour sessions hosted on my server, where the gameplay is constantly driven by live viewer votes that directly impact the game and influence the match. It mostly served my inner circle to run dedicated community nights between 2019 and 2022.
- **[Cobblemon Pasture Loot](https://modrinth.com/mod/cobblemon-pasture-loot):** A highly popular automation mod generating millions of downloads, allowing players to build working resource farms with their pastured Pokémon.
- **[Leon Le Robot Mod](https://modrinth.com/mod/leon-le-robot):** Bringing the stream's infamous mascot directly into the game as an interactive custom mob.
- **[Server Rules](https://modrinth.com/mod/server-rules) & [Community Leaders](https://modrinth.com/mod/community-leaders):** Advanced utility mods designed for large multiplayer servers to enforce rules acceptance and delegate hierarchical staff permissions.

#### 🪵 Rust: RouilleRP (2021)
- **Creator, Core Developer, and Administrator** of *RouilleRP*, a massive persistent French roleplay server built on *Rust*.
- Designed and deployed the entire technical infrastructure and modding ecosystem to transform a brutal PvP survival game into a stable, 100+ player political and narrative simulation.
- Participated on-stream by bringing the main storyline character **Emile Xam** to life.

#### 🏥 The GTA V / FiveM RAGE Engine Era
- **MLO & Interior Reverse Engineering (2015-2016):** Contributed to the pioneering early community efforts to reverse-engineer how MLOs (interiors) behave within Rockstar's RAGE engine in GTAV. Created the original open-source [Central Medical Los Santos MLO](https://www.gta5-mods.com/maps/central-medical-los-santos-interior-mlo) hospital interior to demonstrate native multi-room rendering, laying the structural groundwork for modern GTA RP mapping.
- **[RAGE Photo Manager](https://github.com/dremixam/rage-photo-manager):** A standalone open-source desktop utility allowing *GTA V* and *Red Dead Redemption 2* players to instantly extract and manage their local in-game camera snapshots on PC, completely bypassing Rockstar's online social platform constraints.

#### 🌐 Web, Tools & Transmedia
- **[La COGIP](https://cogip.tv):** Founder of this collaborative streaming network and event collective, built as a mutual-aid hub for French-speaking content creators sharing progressive left-wing values.
- **[PurpleSky](https://github.com/dremixam/purplesky):** A custom automation tool designed for the decentralized **Bluesky** network. It listens to the Twitch API and dynamically updates the user's Bluesky avatar and profile with a "LIVE" badge whenever a stream starts. Follow the updates on my official profile: [@dremixam.com](https://bsky.app/profile/dremixam.com).
- **Clyde Vanilla (2017):** Lead web developer for Antoine Daniel’s audio fiction project, handling the main portal where episodes were published and building the dedicated community forum.
- **[The Liberty-Tree](https://liberty-tree.net):** A historic French-speaking parodic news and text-RP portal set in the GTA universe. Originally launched in 2001, it features over 900 articles archived during a major overhaul in 2016. The site remains online as a historical record, highlighted by Rockstar Games on their *Newswire* in 2012.
